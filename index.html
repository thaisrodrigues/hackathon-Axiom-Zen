Who we are

The Cafu Foundation is a nonprofit, generating development opportunities that operates in combating social inequality. Proposes an educational partner care for about 630 children and low-income youth aged 4-17 years and 11 months and 279 people over 18 years with professional courses. The first center is located in the Jardim Irene, neighborhood where Cafu spent his childhood and for which has a great affection. There started the realization of the project "Feeding Dreams".
